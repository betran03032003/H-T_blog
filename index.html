THE H&T BLOG

Where we enjoy everything!

Welcome everyone to visit and stop at H&T blog. We are Nguyen Lan Huong and Do Ngoc Huyen Tran - the owners of this cute blog. H&T stands for Huong and Tran, in addition, it also has another meaning: "House technological" or "Happy technology". H&T blog is like a special diary of the two of us, it keeps our typical activities at Tay Nguyen University. The place to share knowledge as well as hobbies of Huong and Tran. We write about my study, daily life, hobbies,.... Therefore, you can discover many interesting things about us here. What's more, you can also become a member and start contributing and building with us to create an ever more magical H&T blog.

Where the sky is filled with thoughts. Listen. Think. Read and feel with H&T blog.
